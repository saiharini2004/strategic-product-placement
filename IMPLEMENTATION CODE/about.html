About the Project

Dataset Information

This project utilizes a comprehensive retail product placement dataset that captures critical factors influencing sales performance. The dataset includes:

  • Product Category: Classification of products into different retail segments
  • Product Position: Physical placement location within the store
  • Price: Current selling price of the product
  • Competitor Price: Pricing data from competing retailers
  • Sales Volume: Quantity of products sold within specific timeframes
  • Promotion: Marketing and promotional activities applied to products
  • Demographics: Customer demographic information and purchasing patterns

  • Promotion Impact: Measurement of promotional activities on sales volume
  • Interactive Filters: Dynamic filtering by category, time period, and demographics

Three comprehensive stories provide deep insights into different aspects of product placement strategy:

Story 1: Category Performance

Analyzes how different product categories perform across various placement positions, identifying top-performing segments and optimization opportunities.

Story 2: Placement Impact

Examines the direct correlation between product positioning and sales performance, revealing optimal placement strategies for maximum visibility.

Story 3: Combined Impact

Integrates category performance, placement impact, and promotional effectiveness to provide a holistic view of product placement optimization.
